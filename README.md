A streamlined dividend and ETF tracking app designed to monitor monthly payouts, analyze fund performance, and simulate reinvestment strategies. Built for investors seeking consistent cash flow and smart financial insights.

---

## 🚀 Features

- **Monthly Dividend Dashboard**  
  Track your income by ticker, month, and total flow.

- **ETF Comparison Tool**  
  Analyze yield, sector exposure, expense ratios, and historical performance.

- **Dividend Reinvestment Simulator**  
  Project your portfolio growth over time with customizable compounding logic.

- **Watchlist & Alerts**  
  Stay ahead of ex-dividend dates, payout changes, and ETF rebalances.
